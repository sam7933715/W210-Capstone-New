Visualization

User Travel Analysis

The interactive visual analysis covered here caters to a supervisor user persona. Given a supervisor with a team of sales professionals, these analyses provide a cumulative view of how the team is performing. Following are some examples: the cumulative view of the team spending time on the road to get to their clients. time-traveled vs distance-traveled a view of the above by days of a week

Traveling Distance Detail

The following graph provides a cross filter to help a supervisor slice and dice the historical travel route data from a different perspective. The 4 histograms represent the following:
  • The total number of days a salesperson traveled
  • The selection of specific days on which the analysis is being performed
  • Total number of minutes traveled by users by specific days
  • Total distance traveled in miles by users by specific days
